Government Launches Anaemia Screening Drive in Rural Rajasthan
The Health Department of Rajasthan has launched a large-scale anaemia screening campaign across rural districts including Jaipur, Alwar, and Dausa. Health workers will conduct haemoglobin tests for women and adolescent girls and distribute iron-folic acid supplements through local health centres.
WHO Report Highlights Rising Concerns Around Maternal Health
A recent report released by the World Health Organization highlighted persistent challenges in maternal healthcare access across South Asia. Experts from New Delhi and Mumbai emphasized the importance of regular prenatal checkups, nutritional support, and community awareness programs.
New Nutrition Initiative Targets Iron Deficiency Among Teenagers
The Ministry of Health announced a nutrition initiative in collaboration with UNICEF to address iron deficiency among teenage girls. The program will begin in Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and Madhya Pradesh and include school-based awareness campaigns and free supplement distribution.
